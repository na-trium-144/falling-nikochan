- After specifying the video to play in the meta information tab, you can play and stop the song using control buttons such as "Play".
- A time bar is displayed below the control buttons.
  - The top of the bar shows the time in seconds.
  - The bottom shows the time in beats, as well as the BPM setting, speed setting, and time signature setting.
- Also, if notes have been placed, it also shows how many notes have been placed and at what timing.
