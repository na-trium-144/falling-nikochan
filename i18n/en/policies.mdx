# About user information

- Falling Nikochan can be used without user registration. Users are not asked to enter personal information.
- Play records of playing Falling Nikochan are sent to the server anonymously and stored. This does not include personal information such as cookies or IP addresses.
- Play records such as the history of the charts played by users (listed on the "Play" page on the top page) and best scores are stored in the local storage of the user's browser. This information is not sent to the server.
- In Mac Safari and iOS, data stored in the browser, such as play records, history of charts played or created, and passwords for chart editing (described later), will be deleted after 7 days of no access to the website. This is due to Apple's regulations and is not a bug of Falling Nikochan.
- When a user creates and uploads a chart, the user's IP address is collected and stored. It is used for rate limiting of some APIs and may be used for other purposes if the developer deems it necessary, but it will not be provided to third parties.

# About uploading charts

- This service allows anyone to upload charts without registering as a user.
  The chart uploaded by users is stored on Falling Nikochan's server.
- When creating a chart, you can set an editing password.
- The password can be saved in the browser used for editing.
  In this case, you can usually edit without entering a password, but if you change your PC or browser, or delete data stored in your browser, you may be asked to enter a password.
- Local Storage and Cookies are used to store passwords. They are not used to identify personal information.
- Falling Nikochan does not provide a way to reset your password if you forget it.
  Please manage it at your own risk.
